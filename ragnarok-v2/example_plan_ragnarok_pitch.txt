⚡ RAGNARÖK — The Chaos Agent That Stress-Tests Your Business Plan Before the Market Does

RAGNARÖK is a multi-agent AI system that tears apart any business plan, pitch deck,
or product idea from four independent adversarial perspectives simultaneously,
then synthesises a survival verdict and a reborn plan.

You get brutal honesty in 60 seconds. The kind your investors won't give you until it's too late.

─────────────────────────────────────────────
THE AGENT PANTHEON (Norse mythology, obviously)
─────────────────────────────────────────────

🐺 FENRIR       — Market & competitive destruction
                  Attacks TAM claims, pricing, moat, and competitive dynamics.
                  If your market doesn't exist, Fenrir finds out first.

🌊 JÖRMUNGANDR  — Technical & infrastructure destruction  
                  Attacks your tech stack, scalability, build complexity, and cost structure.
                  If your cloud bill exceeds your revenue, Jörmungandr calculated it already.

🔥 SURTR        — People & organisational destruction
                  Attacks team size, hiring plan, culture risk, and founder behaviour.
                  If two people cannot build what you've promised, Surtr will say so.

💀 HEL          — Legal & regulatory destruction
                  Attacks GDPR violations, licensing gaps, and liability exposure.
                  If your pitch deck contains its own compliance confession, Hel will quote it back to you.

👁️ ODIN         — Synthesis & reborn plan
                  Reads all four chaos reports. Issues a RAGNARÖK Score (0–100).
                  Kills the wrong assumptions. Forges a survival plan from the wreckage.

─────────────────────────────────────────────
HOW IT WORKS
─────────────────────────────────────────────

Input:   Any text — pitch deck, business plan, product brief, napkin idea
Process: FENRIR, JÖRMUNGANDR, SURTR, and HEL run in parallel (async)
         Each streams findings directly to a markdown report file in real time
         ODIN reads all four reports and synthesises the final verdict
Output:  Structured multi-agent workspace with individual agent reports + master INDEX.md

Two implementations:
  v1 — Python async agents, rich terminal UI, structured JSON output
  v2 — File-streaming agents, each writes live to .md files (true filesystem-based communication)
